pushes the worst-case top-5 Fock leakage on the |α|=5 row from 4e-6
(marginal by the audit rubric of
[2026-04-21-coh-theta0-det-rabi5x.md §9](../../wp-hasse-reproduction/logbook/2026-04-21-coh-theta0-det-rabi5x.md))

`nmax_used` (nN, ndt) dataset inside each alpha group in
`coastline_v1.h5`; the group scalar `/alpha_5p0.attrs['nmax']` stores
the policy summary (80) for that |α|.

**Drive-LD breaches confirmed** at the small-δt corners as predicted by
§4.1 of the memo: 4 of 6 δt/T_m points at N=3 breach the Ω_eff/ω_m=0.3
ceiling; the breach count decreases monotonically with N, dropping to 0
at N ≥ 48. All breaching cells are retained and hatched on downstream
plots per §4.1.
