Method

Full spin–motion Schrödinger equation in truncated Fock basis. exp(iη(a+a†)) computed exactly via matrix exponentiation. Decoherence: quantum trajectories with T₁, T₂, heating channels. Projection noise: binomial sampling. GPU acceleration via WebGPU when available (matrix multiply kernel); CPU fallback otherwise.
