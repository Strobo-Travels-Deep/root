Method

For each laser detuning δ₀, the simulation: (1) prepares |↓⟩⊗D(α)|0⟩ in a truncated Fock basis; (2) applies Npulses stroboscopic unitary kicks U=exp(−iHeffδt), with optional Lindblad collapse between pulses; (3) computes spin observables and entropy; (4) optionally applies binomial projection noise.

Fock convergence: After each run, population in the top 3 Fock levels is measured. A banner reports whether the truncation is converged (<0.01%), marginal (0.01–1%), or unreliable (>1%), with a recommended Nmax.
Decoherence: Three Lindblad channels — spin decay (1/T₁), pure dephasing (1/T₂ − 1/(2T₁)), and motional heating (dn/dt via a†) — implemented via quantum-trajectory (Monte Carlo wavefunction) method, averaged over Ntraj trajectories.
Projection noise: Each spin expectation value is binomially sampled with Nrep trials, modelling quantum projection noise in a real experiment. Error bars show ±1σ shot noise.
